How do I place an order?

Add items to cart or tap the WhatsApp button on any product to ask about availability and delivery.

Can I get help choosing my size?

Yes. Use the size guide or message KLINE-MEN on WhatsApp before ordering.

Do you offer delivery?

Delivery information should be clearly shown, with WhatsApp support for confirming location and cost.

Can I exchange an item?

State the exchange period, condition of items, and any exclusions in simple terms.
